Texture Packs
  1. Create a repository for your texture pack: https://github.com/new
  2. Add your images to the repository
  3. In the texture pack manager click the hamburger menu and click create
  4. Insert all the required urls
  5. Click "Download one click file"
  6. Add your one click file to the repository
  7. DM flines#0891 asking to submit your texture pack
